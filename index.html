Project Description and Motivation

Our project was inspired by the SpaceVR project. SpaceVR is "the worldâ€™s first virtual reality platform allowing users to be an astronaut and experience space firsthand." Our project is just that, but with a focus on moon exploration. We wanted to simulate a full moon landing experience, with some futuristic elements. In this setting, the user is the leader of a nine-ship squad traveling to the moon. The experience is broken down into two scenes: the Moon Orbit scene and the Moon Landing Scene.

Moon Orbit Scene (Brett)

This scene is the prelude to the moon landing. The user is hovering, along with eight other squad members, in the Moon's orbit. Visible in all 360 degrees is a rotating asteroid field, and a belt of small, spinning asteroids are wrapped around the moon (this is the future!). On the front of the ship is a panel with five buttons, four to send other ships in, two at a time, and the final to send the user in. The scene lasts as long as the user chooses, ending as soon as the "Self Launch" button is pressed, but the intended progression is to press them in the following order: Squadron One, Two, Three, Four, then Self-Launch.

Technical Breakdown

Here are all the technical components of the project. As before, each scene will be broken down to explain individual pieces.

Moon Orbit Scene (Brett)

Skybox: "Between Moon and Earth 2" (Stagit East - Earth & Planets Skyboxes)

Space fighters: "Omega Fighters" (Omega Creative - Sci-Fi Spaceship)

  • Animations did not come with this package, I created two unique to each ship (18 total).

Asteroids: "Asteroid Set" (solarforge - Asteroid Set)

  • Three sizes of asteroids: small, medium and large, each with random animation
  • Animations did not come with this package, I created three rotations (one for each axis)
  • Broken down into Asteroid Field (cube-shaped area around fighters), and Asteroid Belt around moon

GUI: Heavily modified version of the GUI from "Intro" (Unity - VR Samples)

  • Launch panels are clickable sliders with preset duration (30 for squadrons, 0.00001 for self launch)
  • Pressing panels swaps space fighter animations (hover -> launch), and plays audio clip when complete

Lighting: "Sun1" (Stagit East - Earth & Planets Skyboxes)

Audio: Mirror's Edge Soundtrack - Introduction by Solar Fields
